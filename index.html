Our Services

Beyond Installation

Comprehensive lift services covering testing, certification, and ongoing maintenance for all manufacturers.

LOLER Testing

Thorough examinations under the Lifting Operations and Lifting Equipment Regulations. Full certification provided to ensure legal compliance for all your lifting equipment.

BREEAM Assessments

Environmental performance assessments for your lift installations. We help ensure your building meets sustainability targets and green building standards.

Maintenance & Repairs

Planned preventive maintenance programmes and rapid-response call-outs. We service lifts from all major manufacturers across Northern Ireland, Ireland, and the UK.
